Visit Hyderabad

Enjoy culture and food from one of the most distinct cities in the country

One of the few popular things are

Biryani

Hyderabadi biryani is a dish of rice and meat or vegetables, originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is made with spices, rice, and meat or vegetables, and is often served with raita.

Planetarium

Birla Planetarium Hyderabad: Located at Naubat Pahad in the heart of Hyderabad city, it is one of the most modern planetariums of the country.

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument and mosque located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was commissioned by Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah, the fifth ruler of the Qutb Shahi dynasty, in 1591.
